- This logo was first ideated on paper, with multiple alternative designs
- The design was narrowed down after careful consideration, and for the following reasons
    ## PROS
    1. Very simple design, recognizable from a distance
    2. The logos will be rendered at small scales, and viewed at a divvernce so it should be relatively low detail and simple - no complicated iconography
    3. Very simple color pallete - a single base color of blue and 2 different shades of this same blue
    ## CONS
    However there are some issues I have with this iteration
    1. The colors feel kind of unsaturated. They dont pop out nearly as much as I would want, and especially because this logo will be seen from a distance this is definetly important
    2. When you reduce the size of the logo, it feels very 'unbounded'. Perhaps it will benifit a lot more from a stronger border
