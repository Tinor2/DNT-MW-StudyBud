- This new design improves on the problems of the old one, while still keeping the same kehy features
    ## PROS
    1. The color saturation issue previously mentioned has been solved
        - This was done by switching from a palette comprising colors of the same HUE, and different VALUES, into a palette of colours DIFFERENT HUES, and SIMILIAR VALUES - or analogous colors
        - I also increased the amount of colors being used, from just 3 (one for midtones, highlights and shadows each) into using 6 different colors (3 for shadows, one for midtones and two for higlights)
    2. The 'bounding' issue was solved, by increasing the emphasis on the border DRASTICALLY 
        - Although zoomed in the border can feel a bit much, zoomed out, it feels a lot more natural and much better defined
    ## CONS
    'There definetly is still room for improvement in later iterations'
    1. The shape of the water droplet COULD be iterated more
    2. Maybe the new borders are on a little bit of the too strong side - the shape of the droplet is being overshadowed.
    3. Ive definetly lost a bit of the 'tip' of the droplet. The borders there at least are masking the original shape of it.
